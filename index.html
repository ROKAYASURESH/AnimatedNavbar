ABOUT US

"Welcome to website, a platform dedicated to promoting sustainable living and environmental conservation. My name is Suresh Rokaya, and I am the creator of this website. I have always been passionate about the environment and the impact of human actions on it. I have a degree in Environmental Science and a background in sustainable development. I have worked with several organizations to promote sustainable practices and raise awareness about environmental issues. I started this website as a way to share my knowledge and inspire others to take action to protect our planet. Through website, I aim to provide valuable information and resources on sustainable living, eco-friendly practices, and conservation efforts. I also hope to create a community of like-minded individuals who are committed to creating a better future for our planet. Join me on this journey as we work together to make a positive impact on the environment."
